EST. 1994 — Local Roots

Born from the Earth, Refined by Time.

Monolith began in a small timber workshop overlooking the rugged northern coastline. Inspired by the resilience of the landscape and the honesty of raw materials, we set out to create clothing that endures. Every stitch is a dialogue between heritage and modernity.

The Art of the Slow Process

We reject the pace of fast fashion. Our pieces take time to manifest, ensuring the highest standard of structural integrity.
